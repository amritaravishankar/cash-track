Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

> What is CashTrack?

CashTrack provides an integrated platform solution that enables users to collectively record, analyse and settle their monetary transactions between each other


> Does CashTrack have integrated payment solutions?

No, CashTrack currently does not have any integrated payment solutions or ongoing partnerships with any payments vendors. All transactions must be conducted via third party vendors.


> How is my data protected?

When possible, encryption is used, both in transit and storage. Access controls within the organisation limit who may access information.
